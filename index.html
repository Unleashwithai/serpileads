⚙️ How this tool works

SerpApi searches Google Maps for businesses in your chosen area + category. Then this tool checks each business's website for: load speed, mobile-friendliness signals, SSL, age indicators, and whether it's just a social media redirect. Results are scored and ranked as leads.
